From I-5 South Bound

  1. go through downtown Seattle under the freeway lid
  2. take the right hand I-90 exit toward Bellevue/Spokane
  3. follow signs for I-5 South/Portland/Tacoma
  4. take the right hand exit for Airport Way South
  5. turn right at Lander street
  6. turn right at Occidental Avenue South
